TITLE: Gallup High School
============================================================

[ORG] (GHS) is a public high school in [GPE], [GPE]. [ORG] is the largest high school in the Gallup-McKinley County School District. The school's campus is relatively new as [ORG] moved to its current location in 1998 and the old campus became known as [ORG], which housed 8th and 9th grade students from 1998 to 2008. In the 2008–2009 school year, [ORG] started splitting their 9th and 10th grade students with cross-town [ORG]. The [ORG] attendance boundary after the split will be [ORG]'s westside and areas located west and north of town.
