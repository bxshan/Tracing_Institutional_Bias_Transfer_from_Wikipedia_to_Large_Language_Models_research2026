[ORG] opened in 1973 as a professional arts training center for secondary school-age children. Located in [GPE], it provides intensive instruction in [ORG], creative writing, dance, media arts, music (classical, jazz, vocal), [ORG] (drama, musical theatre, theatre design), and visual arts.
