TITLE: West High School (Salt Lake City)
============================================================

[ORG] is a public high school in [GPE], [GPE]. A part of [GPE], the school serves students in the western part of the city. Founded in 1890 as [ORG], it is one of the oldest public high schools in [GPE]. As of 2026, the school is housed in its historic 1922 building on [GPE] Union Square, however ground was broken for a new building in March 2026.
With an enrollment over 2,500 students, its athletic teams compete in [ORG] region six of the 5A classification. The school's colors are red and black, with a black panther as the mascot.
