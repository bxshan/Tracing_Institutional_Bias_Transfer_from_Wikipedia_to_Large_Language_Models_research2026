Paul Laurence Dunbar High School (also known as Dunbar High School, PLD, or PLDHS) is a public high school located at 1600 Man o' War Boulevard on the southwest side of Lexington, Kentucky, United States. The school is one of six high schools in the Fayette County Public Schools district.
The school was opened in 1990. With an enrollment of over 2100 students (in 2008–2009), it is one of the largest public high schools in Kentucky, and houses the Math, Science, and Technology Center program.
